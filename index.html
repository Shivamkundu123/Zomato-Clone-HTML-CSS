Food quality testing is the process of checking food products to ensure they are safe, nutritious, fresh, and suitable for consumption. It helps maintain standards related to taste, appearance, hygiene, and nutritional value.

food code

Snacks (Chatpata)

Snacks are small meals eaten between regular meals to satisfy hunger and boost energy. They can be healthy or indulgent, depending on choice and occasion. Healthy snacks like fruits, nuts, yogurt, and sprouts provide essential nutrients and help maintain good health, while snacks such as samosas, chips, biscuits, and chocolates are popular for taste and convenience

Breakfast (Subah Ka Nashta)

Breakfast is the first and most important meal of the day, as it provides the energy needed to start daily activities. A healthy breakfast improves concentration, boosts metabolism, and helps maintain overall health

Dinner (Raat Ka Khana)

A healthy dinner may include vegetables, rice or roti, dal, soup, and a small portion of protein like paneer
